UNIVERSAL DESIGN

Why Universal Design Matters

Before Mac and Windows interfaces, computers had command terminals. These terminals required high levels of skill to be used well. Now, most anyone can turn on a computer and start up the Internet or a game, because the terminal is made for everyone.

Our society is currently in the “command terminal” days of public robots. Robots For All is bring us to the Windows of robots.
