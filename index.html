ROBOTS FOR ALL

Robots will be everywhere.

  • In our homes, vacuuming, folding laundry, as pets or companions
  • In public spaces: restaurant waitstaff, hotel assistance, delivery robots

Robots are currently not built for everyone.

  • Must be usable by people of different skills, sizes
  • Most companies aren’t prepared to develop usable robots
  • Robot guidelines still need to be developed

That’s what Robots For All is for.

  • We specialize in improving usability of robotic technologies
  • We partner with cross-domain experts
  • Because more usable robots increases automation acceptance and safety in commercial and business settings.
