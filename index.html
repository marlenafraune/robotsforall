Why it matters

Robots will be everywhere. They need to work for everyone.

From home companions to restaurant staff, robots are entering every aspect of daily life — but most aren't built with inclusive design in mind.

The Opportunity

  • In our homes: vacuuming, laundry, pets, and companions
  • In public: restaurant waitstaff, hotel assistants, delivery robots

The Problem

  • Most robots don't account for different skills and body sizes
  • Companies lack the expertise to develop truly usable robots
  • Industry-wide guidelines for inclusive robotic design are still missing

Our Solution

  • We specialize in improving the usability of robotic technologies
  • We partner with cross-domain experts across HCI, engineering, and accessibility
  • Because more usable robots drive automation acceptance and safety
